BK's Cupcakes

The skinny get fat

In 1902, the shop opened with three people, making fancy, fancy cupcakes. In 1910, we almost shut down, but then Brandon, the owner, came up with a new innovative way to frost the cupcakes. Then in 1915, we became the number one cupcake store in the nation. Now in 2017, we've still kept that reputation.
